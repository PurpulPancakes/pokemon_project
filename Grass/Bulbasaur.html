Bulbasaur フシギダネ (001)

Bulbasaur is a small, quadruped Pokémon that has blue-green skin with darker patches. It has red eyes with white pupils, pointed, ear-like structures on top of its head, and a short, with a wide mouth. A pair of small, pointed teeth are visible in the upper jaw when its mouth is open. Each of its thick legs ends with three sharp claws,on its back is a green plant bulb. The bulb provides it with energy through photosynthesis as well as from the nutrient-rich seeds contained within.

EVOLUTION
